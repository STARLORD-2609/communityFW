About the Project

In today’s digital world, young adults are highly dependent on online platforms for communication, education, and financial transactions. However, this increased usage also exposes them to cyber threats such as phishing, identity theft, and data breaches. Many users lack awareness about safe online practices, which increases their vulnerability.

This project focuses on the design and development of a web-based cybersecurity awareness assessment system. It evaluates users’ knowledge through a structured quiz and provides personalized feedback to improve their awareness level.

Problem Statement

Despite the rapid growth of digital technologies, cybersecurity awareness has not developed at the same pace. Many users are unaware of risks such as weak passwords, phishing attacks, and unsafe data sharing practices. There is a need for an interactive system that helps users assess their awareness and learn safe online behavior.

Objectives

  • To assess cybersecurity awareness among young adults
  • To educate users about online safety practices
  • To provide personalized feedback and suggestions
  • To promote responsible digital behavior

Project Features

  • Interactive quiz with 15 questions
  • Score calculation with awareness levels
  • Graph and animated result display
  • Personalized cyber safety suggestions
  • Detailed awareness information page
