Cybersecurity Awareness

Cybersecurity Awareness System

In today’s digital age, the internet plays a vital role in the daily lives of young adults. From social media interaction and online education to digital payments and cloud services, people are highly dependent on digital platforms. While these technologies provide convenience and efficiency, they also expose users to various cybersecurity threats such as phishing, identity theft, data breaches, and malware attacks. Many users lack awareness about safe online practices, which increases their vulnerability to cyber risks. Common issues include the use of weak passwords, ignoring software updates, clicking on suspicious links, and sharing personal information without caution. This project focuses on the design and development of a web-based cybersecurity awareness assessment system for young adults. The platform allows users to enter basic details such as name and age and then attempt a structured questionnaire consisting of multiple-choice questions. The system evaluates user responses and calculates a score based on their awareness level. Based on the results, users are categorized into different levels such as low, moderate, and high awareness. The platform also provides feedback and suggestions to help users improve their cybersecurity knowledge and adopt safer online practices. The main objective of this project is to create an interactive and user-friendly system that not only assesses awareness but also educates users and promotes responsible digital behavior in the community.
